Registration for SALT32 is now open. Please register before May 8, 2022.

Payment (all fees are in USA dollars)

Please keep a copy of your payment receipt (if applicable). You will need it to complete your registration. Note that all coauthors must register as presenters.

  • Online presenters (Students, part-time and unemployed): FREE

  • Online attendees: FREE

  • Online presenters (Full-time employed –including postdocs– and all others): $50

  • In-person presenters (Students, part-time and unemployed): $130

  • In-person presenters (Full-time employed –including postdocs– and all others): $180
The fee for in-person presenters covers 3 small breakfasts, coffee services, 3 lunches, and the conference dinner (excluding alcoholic drinks).

SALTED Access and Inclusivity Fund

SALTED has established an Access and Inclusivity Fund. The fund will be used to underwrite sign language translation and other accessibility-related costs at SALT conferences that need it. If you feel like you have the means, the SALTED committee would appreciate a contribution from you. For more information, please visit the SALTED webpage. Note that this contribution is independent of the conference fee and will be administered by the SALTED committee.
