SALT(ED) Workshop

As a simultaneous event to SALT32, the SALT Committee on Equity and Diversity, SALT(ED), and the SALT32 organizing committee will be holding a workshop on issues such as fieldwork ethics, the experience of non-native English speakers as academic semanticists, and the dissemination of non-English academic work in English academia. The exact date and format of this workshop are yet to be determined.
