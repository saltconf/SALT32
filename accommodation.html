Accommodation near the venue

El Colegio de México is situated in the south of Mexico City, next to the “periférico”, the main highway of the city. There are some good hotels nearby, which are very convenient distance-wise. However, please note that they are not located in a lively area with restaurants to dine or walk around. These hotels are:

    Address: Anillo Periférico (Blvd Adolfo Ruiz Cortines) 4363 Col. Jardines En La Montaña Deleg, Tlalpan, 14210 Ciudad de México, CDMX.
    Phone: 52 55 5449 4000

Accommodation in Coyoacán (around 10 km from the venue)

Alternatively, there are some other options in Coyoacán. Coyoacán is a historic borough also in the south of the City (but please bear in mind the size of Mexico City). There are plenty of places to dine, beautiful streets, and historic sites (again, please do not walk at night). In the morning, it should take you 25-35 minutes to get to our venue by taxi or Uber, and the fare should be around $80-120 Mexican Pesos ($4-6 US dollars), depending on the traffic. There are no big hotels in Coyoacán, but there are some convenient B&B options.
